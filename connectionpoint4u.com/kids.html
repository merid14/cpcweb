  • KidzConnect DNA

    We have created an atmosphere that is super funand  the teaching is specifically designed for children ages pre-school through 5th grade in order to lead children into  a life tranforming relationship with Jesus! How is this done? We believe it is done in 3 simple ways.

    Developing:

    Our children are constantly growing and developing. We believe that God has put us here to help your child develop a relationship with Christ. We desire for them to develop into the children that God desires for them to be, so they can be used by Him to forever change the world! So we teach the the Word in a fun and relevant way!

    Nurturing:

    We believe that every child that walks through our doors should be engaged in a simple, safe, and fun environment. The greatest way to nurture a child is with LOVE!!! And believe me our kidz connect volunteers LOVE KIDZ!!! Love in itself can change lives...that's why we are asking you to let us love your kidz!!!

    Applying:

    We have seen a common mistake in churches all over the world. This mistake is that churches don't teach the children how to apply what they have learned. Here at Connection Point, we seek to help children learn how to make the lesson "stick". This is done by giving them practical ways to carry out the words of Jesus into their everyday lives

    When you are in the car and heading home or out to eat after service ask you child 2 questions: 1. Did you have fun? 2. What did you learn?   Then don't be afraid to share with us how that goes!!!
