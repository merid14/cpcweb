WHAT TO EXPECT DURING OUR SUNDAY WORSHIP EXPERIENCE

Things You May Want To Know?

"What time do services start?"   We are super excited to announce we are going to two services starting easter sunday 9/16/2017. For you early birds our first service will start at 9:15 AM. Our second service will start at the same time it always has 11:04 AM! 

"What should I wear?"  COME AS YOU ARE!!! We are super casual and layed back here at Connection Point Church

What should we expect when we pull in; where do we go?   We have heard too many stories of people who pull up to a church, but they couldn't bring themselves to walk in the door. This why you will be greeted and directed where to park by one of our "parking attendents". We love these guys!  After you get yourself parked, get the kids out of the car, our parking crew will point in the right direction!

 Then you will be warmly welcomed as you enter. We will show you where to go in order to check your kids into Kidz Connect and to where you can start off with a coffee and a donut before the service.

Coffee and Donuts:

Maybe you struggle getting up, getting ready, and being on time in the morning. We completely understand...that's why we made it a bit easy for you. We have coffee, drinks, donuts and other snacks waiting on you so you don't have to worry about that part of your morning before you leave the house.

 

Kidz Connect/Nursery:

Sign your children safely into our KIDZ CONNECT Ministry for ages pre-school through 5th grade. This is an atmosphere we have created specifically to CONNECT this age group to a life transforming relationship with Jesus! Our nursery is fully equipped and ready with people who want to simply love your kiddos! All volunteers are required to go through a process that includes background checks and proper training based on the ages of children they will be engaging with. So know they will be safe, loved, and taught about Jesus!

 Worship:

You can expect a 70 minute service loaded with dynamic engaging music, followed up with practical, and relevant teaching. The church is never supposed to be boring or predictable...so to be frank...we have a blast!! Our desire is that not only those who are following Jesus would enjoy and embrace a worship experience here, but that those who are far from God, or those with NO church experience, would also have a great experience!
