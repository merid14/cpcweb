Money Talks

For too many, times are hard, money is tight, and jobs are few. Surviving today, saving for tomorrow, and living with the future in mind requires us to have a grip on our finances! We will either manage our money or our money will manage us. So, in this moment..IF your MONEY could TALK what would yours say about you! Let's make it good!

Money Talks Week Three
5-18-14  |  Pastor Daniel Payne

So many of us are burdened down with debt. One of the ways our money manages us is in the world of debt. Let's look at some practical ways to make sure our debt doesn't manage us, but that we manage it.

Special Sunday: Don't Listen to Discouragement
5-11-14  |  Pastor Jeremiah Evans

Discouragement will derail us faster from the Plan of God then so many things. Listen in on this Mother's Day sermon preached by Jeremiah to find out how we can battle against the voice of discouragement!

Money Talks Week Two
5-4-14  |  Pastor Jeremiah Evans

What is your primary objective when it comes to your personal finances? For some it's supporting a family; others strive to save as much as possible. For many, financial freedom is the finish line. What should your objective be when it comes to your money? What will bring you the most peace? Join Jeremiah Evans as he explores the Bible to find the answer. It just might surprise you.

Money Talks Week One
4-27-14  |  Pastor Jeremiah Evans

Are you financially balanced? How do you know? If you don't know where your money goes each month, you're financially unbalanced. If you are married and argue with your spouse about money, you're financially unbalanced. If your month lasts longer than your money . . . In this message, Jeremiah Evans reveals the three laws of financial balance and discusses how they apply to your life. Learn the first step toward achieving the freedom and contentment that comes with being balanced.
