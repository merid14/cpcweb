CONNECT

There is a spiritual battle going on that, although we cannot see, it is never ending. I believe the 3 connections that seem to be fought against the most by the evil one is our relationship with God, our relationships with others, and with our relationships with ourselves! In life we always tend to find our focus on what is not working, knowing there is a bad connection, but sometimes not knowing the wiring system, or in other words, how God is wired, and it prevents us from seeing our faults and our short comings, and WHERE WE ARE DISCONNECTED!! It's time we fight back...and CONNECT!

Master Connection Week One
11-03-12, Pastor Jeremiah

What is it that seems to disonnect us from God? Does God even really WANT to connect with us? How can we plug in to the full Power of God?

People 2 People Week Two
11-10-13, Pastor Jeremiah

What is it that seems to disonnect us from other people? Are we suppose to do it on our own, or with others? If we are going to make lasting CONNECTIONS with others it must be with a heart of denying self! And we have to realize we were created for realationships...our whole lives are about the people who are in them!

Identity Crisis Week Three
11-17-13, Pastor Jeremiah

When in Christ your present circumstances do not determine your identity…Your IDENTITY is NOT defined by what you DO it is defined by WHO THE FATHER SAYS YOU ARE! You are Christ's MASTERPIECE, you are BRAND NEW, and you are VICTORIOUS in Christ!

What do you have to offer? Week Four
11-24-13, Pastor Jeremiah

In connecting with God, you are able to acknowledge your identity in Christ, which allows you to do life with people the way Go had intended you to. But now what? What do you have to offer? I believe it is much more then you will ever fully realize. YOUR WHOLE LIFE! He wants all of you in order to play your role in this life and in the church and to work hard in serving the Lord Enthusiastically!
